About Us

Welcome to DEBSOC, the heartbeat of discourse at IIEST Shibpur. Here, minds don't just meet they ignite. We invite you to embark on a journey where intellect is the compass, eloquence is the craft, and rhetoric is elevated to an art form.

Beyond the podium, we offer a sanctuary for the curious to sharpen their wit and master the power of persuasion. Join our league of thinkers and let your voice echo through the corridors of discovery.

About BECON

BECON (Bengal Engineering Conclave of Nations) is the flagship Model United Nations conference organised by The Debating Society of IIEST Shibpur.

Step into the world of diplomacy, lobbying, geopolitics and high-stakes negotiation where every delegate holds the power to influence global narratives.
